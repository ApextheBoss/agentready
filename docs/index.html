Why This Matters

AI agents are becoming the primary way users interact with web content — through ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and thousands of autonomous agents. Websites that aren't agent-friendly are becoming invisible, the same way non-mobile-friendly sites disappeared after 2015.

The $47B AI agent market (projected 2030) runs on web content. But only 4 out of 66 major tech sites scored above a D. The infrastructure isn't ready.
